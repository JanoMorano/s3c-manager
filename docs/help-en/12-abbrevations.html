12 Abbreviations

Glossary of abbreviations, terms and statuses

This chapter explains frequent terms used in S3C Manager, ITIL, TOGAF, C3, FMN, APIs and workflow states. It helps managers, administrators and architects use the same vocabulary.

Application terms

TermMeaning
ServiceManaged catalogue item with owner, lifecycle, offering, support and governance context.
Offering evidenceConsumer-facing proof of what can be requested or consumed.
ReadinessSet of checks showing whether a service is ready for publication, change or operation.
DecisionAuditable governance record such as approve, reject, defer or exception.
CapabilityBusiness or mission ability supported by services and C3 elements.
C3Command, Control and Communication taxonomy context.
FMN spiralBaseline grouping used for capability and C3 context.

ITIL and ITSM

TermMeaning
SLAService Level Agreement.
OLAOperational Level Agreement.
Service catalogueAuthoritative user-facing list of services.
Change enablementControlled process for evaluating and approving changes.
Continual improvementOngoing cycle of reviewing and improving services.

TOGAF, EA and ArchiMate

TermMeaning
EAEnterprise Architecture.
Capability mapStructured map of organizational or mission capabilities.
Application componentArchitecture element representing an application.
Data objectArchitecture element representing data used or produced by services.

Workflow statuses

StatusMeaning
DraftService is being prepared and is not ready for normal consumption.
ActiveService is published or operational.
RetiringService is being phased out.
RetiredService is no longer active.
ApprovedReviewer accepted the decision scope.
DeferredDecision is postponed.
ExceptionKnown gap or risk is accepted under documented conditions.

How to use the glossary

When a cockpit badge or field name is unclear, open the glossary first, then continue to the chapter for that application area. This keeps users aligned on the same meaning before they change data or record a decision.
